Garage-Specific Coverage

Garagekeepers

The most critical protection for your shop. Covers physical damage to your customers' vehicles while they are in your care, custody, or control—whether on the lift or parked in the lot overnight.

Garage Liability

Protecting your business from third-party claims. This covers bodily injury or property damage resulting from your garage operations, including accidents during test drives.

Tools & Equipment

Your mechanics invest heavily in their livelihood. We structure property coverage to protect expensive diagnostic equipment, lifts, and the highly valuable tools your technicians own.

Workers Comp

Auto repair is physical, hazardous work. Protect your technicians and your bottom line by covering medical costs and lost wages for work-related injuries in the bay.

Commercial Property

Protecting the physical structure of your shop, the heavy equipment bolted to the floor, and your office assets against fire, severe weather, and unforeseen disasters.

Cyber Liability

Modern shops run on data. We protect your business from the financial fallout of data breaches, ransomware targeting your diagnostic software, and compromised customer payment information.
