Garage-Specific Coverage

Garage Liability

The foundation of your protection. This covers bodily injury or property damage resulting from your garage operations, including accidents during test drives or "slip and fall" incidents in the shop.

Garagekeepers

Protects your customers' vehicles while they are in your care. We help you navigate the difference between Legal Liability and Direct Primary coverage, so you aren't forced to tell a customer "no" after an incident.

Tools & Equipment

Your mechanics invest heavily in their livelihood. We structure property coverage to protect expensive diagnostic equipment, specialized machinery, and the highly valuable tools your technicians own.

Workers Comp

Auto repair is physical, hazardous work. Protect your technicians and your bottom line by covering medical costs and lost wages for work-related injuries in the bay.

Commercial Property

Protecting the physical structure of your shop, your office assets, and your inventory against fire, severe weather, and unforeseen disasters.

Cyber Liability

Modern shops run on data. We protect your business from the financial fallout of data breaches, ransomware targeting your diagnostic software, and compromised customer payment information.
