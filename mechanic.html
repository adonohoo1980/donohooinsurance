The 5-Minute Shop Audit

I spent two decades protecting the largest dealership groups and independent shops in the Midwest. I’ve seen where the gaps hide—usually in the fine print of Garagekeepers limits or diagnostic equipment endorsements.

I don't just sell you a policy; I audit your operation to ensure that if a customer's car is stolen or a tech makes a mistake on a high-end diagnostic, you aren't paying for it out of your own pocket.

Garagekeepers

Coverage for the customer vehicles in your care, custody, and control. I ensure you have "Legal Liability" or "Direct Primary" set up correctly based on your specific risk.

Tools and Equipment

Your shop runs on expensive diagnostic tools and scanners. I make sure your limits aren't just covering the hand tools, but the high-tech equipment that keeps you in business.

Errors and Omissions

Protecting you against claims of faulty workmanship. If a repair fails after the car leaves the lot, you need to know exactly how your policy responds.

Workers Comp

The auto repair industry is physically demanding. I help you manage your mod and protect your team with specialized automotive worker's compensation markets.
